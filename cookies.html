BAKING CHOCO CHIP COOKIES

Step 1: Gather Your Kitchen Materials
  • 1 large mixing bowl
  • 1 small mixing bowl
  • 1 wisk
  • 1 electric mixer
  • Baking Sheet
  • Wire cooling rack
  • Wax or parchment paper
  • Non-Stick cooking spray
  • 1 tablespoon and teaspoon from a set
  • 1 cup
  • ¼ cup


Step 2: Gather Your Ingredients
  • 2¼ cups all purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup (2 sticks) of softened butter
  • ¾ cups granulated sugar
  • ¾ cups brown sugar (packed)
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 cups chocolate chips (either milk, dark, or semi-sweet)


Step 3: Mixing The Ingredients
  • Combining Dry Ingredients:-
    In your smaller mixing bowl, combine your flour, baking soda, and salt.
    Whisk it together and put it to the side.
    Add Chocolate Chips.

  • Combining Wet Ingredients:-
    In your larger mixing bowl, beat together with your electric mixer the granulated sugar and your softened butter until it is creamy.
    Then, once your mixture is light and creamy, add in your brown sugar and vanilla.
    Beat it all together until everything is combined.


Step 4: Add Eggs
  • Add in the eggs one at a time to the butter, sugar, and vanilla mixture after it is all incorporated.
    Beat the mixture after each egg addition. Beat the mixture again once both eggs are mixed in
    Beat this mixture until it is creamy.

Step 5: Bake Your Cookies
  • Preheat your oven to 350° fahrenheit for 15 minutes.
    Cook the cookies for 9 to 11 minutes, or until golden brown.
    When the cookies are done take them out of the oven, off the tray and transfer them to the wire rack.
    Start your next batch with the leftover dough.
